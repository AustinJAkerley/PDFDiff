# Privacy Policy — PDF Diff

_Last updated: 2026-06-26_

PDF Diff is a browser extension that compares two PDF files and highlights the
differences between them. Your privacy is the default: everything happens on
your own device.

## What data we collect

**None.** PDF Diff does not collect, store, transmit, or sell any personal
information or document content.

## How your PDFs are handled

- PDFs you open are processed **entirely within your browser**.
- PDF contents are **never uploaded** and are **not sent to any backend or
  third-party service**.
- The extension uses **no host permissions** and makes **no network requests**
  with your document data.
- No account, login, or cloud sync is used.
- When you close the PDF Diff page, the loaded PDF data is discarded from
  in-memory state. Nothing is persisted.

## Analytics, tracking, and advertising

PDF Diff contains **no analytics, no tracking, and no advertising**. There are
no cookies, fingerprinting, or telemetry of any kind.

## Permissions

PDF Diff requests only the minimum needed to open its own comparison page from
the toolbar button. It does not request access to your browsing activity, your
tabs' contents, or any websites.

## Children's privacy

PDF Diff does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including
children.
